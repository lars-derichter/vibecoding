# Risks of Vibecoding

What you need to know.

---

## Risk 1: You Don't Understand What You Built

<v-clicks>

- If you **can't explain it**, you can't fix it, extend it, or defend it
- You become dependent on AI for every small change
- **Mitigation:** Always ask Claude to explain. Read the code, even if briefly.

</v-clicks>

<v-click>

> Vibecoding is not an excuse to stop thinking.

</v-click>

---

## Risk 2: AI Makes Confident Mistakes

<v-clicks>

- **Hallucinations:** code that looks right but has subtle bugs
- **Security issues:** AI might skip input validation, use outdated patterns
- The code _looks_ professional — that makes mistakes harder to spot

</v-clicks>

<v-click>

**Mitigation:** Test everything. Don't trust blindly. Ask Claude to review its own work.

</v-click>

---

## Risk 3: Dependency and Skill Atrophy

<v-clicks>

- If you **only** vibecode, you never learn to code
- You lose the ability to **evaluate AI output critically**
- This matters when the AI is wrong and you can't tell

</v-clicks>

<v-click>

**Mitigation:** Use vibecoding as a **learning tool**, not a replacement for understanding. Ask "why" often.

</v-click>

---

## Risk 4: Privacy and Intellectual Property

<v-clicks>

- Your code is sent to **Anthropic's servers** (cloud-based)
- **Never paste** credentials, API keys, or sensitive business data
- Check your **organization's AI policy** before using on real projects
- Be aware of **licensing** of generated code

</v-clicks>

<v-click>

> When in doubt: don't paste it. Ask your manager or legal team.

</v-click>
