# Wrap-up

---

## Show and Tell

<v-clicks>

- 2-3 groups volunteer to **show what they built**
- What was your idea?
- What did you prompt?
- What surprised you?

</v-clicks>

---

## Key Takeaways

<v-clicks>

- Vibecoding is a powerful tool for **prototyping and learning**
- **Think first, prompt clearly, verify always**
- Know the risks: don't trust blindly, protect sensitive data
- Claude Code is your **collaborator**, not your replacement

</v-clicks>

---

## Keep Experimenting!

<v-clicks>

- You can **move faster than your current skills suggest** — but reading the diff is still on you
- Start small: a personal website, a simple tool, a prototype for a business idea
- Break things, learn from mistakes, iterate
- The best way to understand AI's strengths and limits is to **use it**

</v-clicks>

<v-click>

> "The future belongs to those who can **collaborate with AI**, not compete with it."

</v-click>
