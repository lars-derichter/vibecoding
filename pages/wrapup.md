## Show and Tell

<v-clicks>

- 2-3 groups volunteer to **show what they built**
- What was your idea?
- What did you prompt?
- What surprised you?

</v-clicks>

---

## Key Takeaways

<v-clicks>

- Vibecoding is a powerful tool for **prototyping and learning**
- **Think first, prompt clearly, verify always**
- Know the risks: don't trust blindly, protect sensitive data
- Claude Code is your **collaborator**, not your replacement

</v-clicks>
